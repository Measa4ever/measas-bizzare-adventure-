Interesting Bio!

Hi there! My name is Measa, glad to meet (virtually hah) you! So lets get down to basics, I'm a 16 year old Junior at The Workshop School. My passions are programming, carpentry, and graphic designing. I love to play games that make me interested on how the game is built and what art work they used. I'm the normal average person who loves to binge watch anime, gameplays, speed art, and occasionally draw. I love to work with my hands and build random things that come up in my head such as tables, logos, banners, anything that's in my grasp I'll try!
